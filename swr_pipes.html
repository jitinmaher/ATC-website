Details on SWR pipes

SWR stands for Soil, waste & rain. They are a perfect solution for the discharge of soil, waste, and rainwater.

These pipes are immune to bacteria, fungi, micro-organisms, rodents and insect attack. SWR pipes are lightweight and can be transported easily so it reduces transportation cost. The low weight of the pipes makes it easy to install and reduces handling and installation costs. The pipes are corrosion- resistant resulting in better flow rate. These pipes are rust proof which confirms longer durability of the product.
